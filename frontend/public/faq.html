Arrival & Check-in

Unfortunately pets are not allowed but guide dogs / assistance dogs are permitted.

If you require an assistance dog, please let us know in advance so we can ensure appropriate arrangements are made.

Since meals and refreshments are included, you'll only need spending money for:

  • Bar drinks (if desired)
  • Books or resources from the resource table
  • Personal snacks or treats
  • Souvenirs or gifts

Most attendees find £20-40 sufficient for the weekend. Card payments are accepted for most purchases.

We understand that circumstances can change. Our cancellation policy is:

  • More than 4 weeks before: Full refund minus £10 admin fee
  • 2-4 weeks before: 50% refund
  • Less than 2 weeks: No refund, but we can transfer your booking to a future retreat

Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to cancel. In exceptional circumstances, we may be able to make special arrangements.

There's no strict dress code - we want you to be comfortable! Here are some guidelines:

  • Sessions: Smart casual (think Sunday church attire)
  • Meals: Casual and comfortable
  • Recreation: Appropriate sportswear if using facilities
  • Evening: Relaxed casual wear

The key is modesty and comfort. Dress as you would for a church weekend away with friends!

  • Shared meals: Informal dining times

We're committed to making the retreat accessible for everyone. Please let us know about any specific needs when you register so we can:

  • Ensure meeting rooms are accessible
  • Provide hearing loop assistance
  • Arrange for any special dietary requirements
  • Coordinate transport assistance if needed

Don't hesitate to contact us - we want to ensure you have a comfortable and meaningful retreat experience.

Registration and check-in begins at 3:00 PM on Friday. We recommend arriving between 3:00 PM and 5:00 PM to allow time to settle in before our welcome session at 6:00 PM.

If you need to arrive earlier due to travel arrangements, please contact us in advance and we'll do our best to accommodate early check-in where possible.

Please check with your conference organiser for your arrival time.

Bedrooms will be available as soon as possible on the day of arrival and must be vacated by 10 AM on the day of departure.

All our centres have ample free parking on site. If you are staying at The Hayes your event organiser should tell you if you are registering in the Main House or Lakeside. For Lakeside registrations, follow signs for Lakeside Accommodation and Parking on arrival.

Detailed directions and address information will be sent to you via email one week before the retreat. The venue is easily accessible by both car and public transport.

If you're traveling by train, we can arrange shared transport from the nearest station - please let us know in advance if you'd like to join a group transfer.

Electric car charging facilities are available at all of our venues on a first-come, first-served basis. Visit reception to pay for a charging session before use. Our chargers typically deliver around 7kW of power and are charged at 50p per kWh (subject to change).

At The Hayes we have four chargers which can be found at the back of the car park to the left of reception as you drive in. There are also two next to Lakeside accommodation.

Accommodation

Your room includes:

  • Bedding: All bed linen is provided
  • Towels: A bath towel and hand towel for each guest
  • Toiletries: Soap, shower gel/shampoo in your bathroom
  • Amenities: Hairdryer, tea/coffee making facilities
  • Connectivity: Fast Wi-Fi access

Please note: Due to the nature of the events we host, we do not have TVs in our bedrooms. However, there are TVs available in common areas of the centres.

Yes! When you register, you can request to share a room with a specific person. Both people must make this request for us to honor it.

If you don't specify a preference, we'll match you with someone of the same gender. We do our best to consider age and interests when making room assignments.

All our main conference halls are accessible for delegates with disabilities and feature hearing aid loops. We have a selection of accessible bedrooms available at each centre.

Important: Please ensure that your conference organiser knows your requirements so that appropriate accommodation may be reserved for you.

Single rooms are available for an additional £40 per night, subject to availability. Please contact us as soon as possible to request a single room as they are limited and allocated on a first-come, first-served basis.

  • Teaching Sessions: Inspirational talks and biblical teachings
  • Workshop Groups: Interactive sessions for personal growth
  • Prayer and Worship: Times of corporate and personal prayer
  • Fellowship Meals: Opportunities to connect with other attendees
  • Free Time: Space for reflection, rest, and informal fellowship
  • Optional Activities: Recreation time using venue facilities

While we encourage full participation to get the most from your retreat experience, we understand that everyone's needs are different. The main teaching sessions and group meals are key parts of the program, but you're free to step out if you need personal time or rest.

We want this to be a refreshing and meaningful time for everyone, so please feel free to participate at a level that feels right for you.

Absolutely! The retreat schedule includes dedicated times for personal reflection and prayer. We've also designed the program with enough space between sessions for quiet contemplation.

Facilities & Services

Yes! You'll find free-of-charge Wi-Fi at all of our venues. This service is available in all meeting rooms, common areas and bedrooms, at speeds of around 10mbps per user.
