Growth and Wisdom Retreat

Frequently Asked Questions - Everything you need to know for your retreat experience

Arrival & Check-in

Registration and check-in begins at 3:00 PM on Friday. We recommend arriving between 3:00 PM and 5:00 PM to allow time to settle in before our welcome session at 6:00 PM.

If you need to arrive earlier due to travel arrangements, please contact us in advance and we'll do our best to accommodate early check-in where possible.

Bedrooms will be available as soon as possible on the day of arrival and must be vacated by 10 AM on the day of departure.

All our centres have ample free parking on site. If you are staying at The Hayes, follow signs for the appropriate parking area based on your accommodation location.

Electric car charging facilities are available on a first-come, first-served basis. Visit reception to pay for charging sessions at 50p per kWh. Chargers deliver around 7kW of power.

Accommodation

Your room includes:

  • Bedding: All bed linen provided
  • Towels: Bath and hand towels
  • Toiletries: Soap, shower gel/shampoo
  • Amenities: Hairdryer, tea/coffee facilities
  • Wi-Fi: Fast internet access

Note: No TVs in bedrooms, but available in common areas.

Food & Drinks

Yes! Our centres are all-inclusive. You'll enjoy:

  • Cooked and continental breakfasts
  • Light lunches
  • Hot evening buffets
  • Tea, coffee, and refreshments during breaks

We provide dishes for vegetarians, vegans, and cater for all 14 recognised allergens including gluten-free, dairy-free, and nut allergies.

Important: Please advise us of requirements before arrival.

Included: Coffee, tea, hot chocolate (8am-8pm), plus in-room facilities. Bar drinks are additional and paid by card.

Activities & Schedule

Our program includes:

  • Teaching Sessions: Inspirational talks and biblical teachings
  • Workshop Groups: Interactive personal growth sessions
  • Prayer and Worship: Corporate and personal prayer times
  • Fellowship Meals: Connect with other attendees
  • Free Time: Reflection, rest, and recreation

We encourage full participation, but understand everyone's needs are different. Main sessions and meals are key parts of the program, but you're free to step out for personal time if needed.

Facilities & Services

Practical Information

Essential items:

  • Bible and notebook
  • Comfortable clothing for sessions
  • Weather-appropriate outerwear
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Personal medications
