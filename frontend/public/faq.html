Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about your retreat experience

Arrival & Check-in

Registration and check-in begins at 4:00 PM on Friday. We recommend arriving between 3:00 PM and 5:00 PM to allow time to settle in before our welcome session at 5:30 PM.

If you need to arrive earlier due to travel arrangements, please contact us in advance and we'll do our best to accommodate early check-in where possible.

Check-in: 3:00 PM - 6:00 PM on Friday

Check-out: By 11:00 AM on Sunday

Late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability.

Accommodation

We offer various accommodation options:

  • Double rooms: Shared rooms with two beds
  • Family rooms: Larger rooms for families

All rooms include fresh linens, towels, and basic amenities.

Meals & Dietary Requirements

All meals are included in your retreat package:

  • Breakfast: 7:30 AM - 9:00 AM
  • Lunch: 12:30 PM - 1:30 PM
  • Dinner: 6:30 PM - 8:00 PM
  • Snacks & beverages: Available throughout the day

Important:These times are subject to change

Yes! We can accommodate most dietary requirements including:

  • Vegetarian and vegan options
  • Gluten-free meals
  • Food allergies and intolerances

Important: Please inform us of any dietary requirements when booking or at least 1 week before arrival.

Activities & Schedule

Our retreat includes a variety of activities:

  • Daily prayer walks
  • Nature walks and outdoor activities
  • Workshop sessions and group discussions
  • Free time for personal reflection

While all activities are optional, we encourage you can participate and use the retreat as an opportunity to build new relationships within the church family

Facilities & Amenities

Our retreat center offers:

  • Wi-Fi: Available in common areas (limited in rooms to encourage digital detox)
  • Library: Quiet space with books and resources
  • Gardens: Beautiful outdoor spaces for reflection
  • Meditation hall: Dedicated space for practice
  • Common areas: Lounges and social spaces

Practical Information

Essential items to pack:

  • Comfortable clothing for activities and relaxation
  • Layered clothing (weather can change)
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Personal toiletries and any medications
  • Journal or notebook (optional)
  • Reusable water bottle

Provided: Linens, towels, beddings.
